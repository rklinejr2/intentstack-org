The Intent Stack is a working specification. We welcome scrutiny, questions, and collaboration from people who work at the intersection of organizational governance and AI deployment.

## Who typically engages

- **Standards body members** evaluating governance architecture for AI agent deployment
- **BPM, BRM, and governance practitioners** extending process, decision, and case management into AI contexts
- **Researchers and academics** studying AI governance frameworks
- **Practitioners** interested in contributing to the specification
